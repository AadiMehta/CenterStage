Connecting Zoom with Centrestage

    1. Create a new Centrestage account
    2. During the sign up process, you will be asked to Connect your Zoom account. Click 'Connect Zoom'
    3. Please enter your existing Zoom credentials and click Confirm

Disconnecting Zoom from Centrestage

    1. Login to your Centrestage Account using your credentials
    2. Go to My Account -> Info
    3. Click Disconnect on Zoom
    4. Click Confirm

ZOOM USAGE

    ▪ Teachers, Trainers or Coaches on Centrestage can use the Zoom SDK integration to conduct their Live Online Lessons
    ▪ Users can connect their existing Zoom accounts from within the Centrestage platform
    ▪ Users will require creating a Zoom account in order to conduct their Live Lessons on Centrestage

UNINSTALLING CENTRESTAGE FROM YOUR ZOOM ACCOUNT

    ▪ Login to your Zoom Account and navigate to the Zoom App Marketplace
    ▪ Click Manage -> Installed Apps or search for the Centrestage app
    ▪ Click the Centrestage App
    ▪ Click Uninstall
